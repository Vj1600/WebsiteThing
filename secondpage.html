What are some things I find amusing?

Playing videogames and listening to music are a small amount of things that I find intriguing. Hearing music will always get me back in good spirits! Lil Uzi Vert is an artist that I enjoy listening to while waiting and anticipating any new music he plans to drop. He's my Favorite Rapper of all time! I'm known for being a fan when it comes to him.

Other artists I recommend are Playboi Carti along with Tyler, The Creator just to name a few. Carti's rapid word use and Tyler's quick-witted references continue to inspire young people like myself, one at a time.

The videogames that I play are combat games that can include a lot of shooting. Personally I prefer Sonic or Kirby games at this time. I consider myself a "Videogame Expert" even at some of the un-popular games that are out now.
